Academic Background

I am a Mathematician (B.S. from UNICEN, Argentina) specializing in the intersection of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Automated Theorem Proving (ATP). My research focuses on auto-formalization: using neural models to bridge the gap between natural language mathematics and formal verification systems like Lean 4 and Coq.

Research Interests

  • Auto-formalization Translating informal mathematical text into formal logic (Lean/Isabelle).
  • Neuro-Symbolic AI Combining neural networks with symbolic reasoning for robust theorem proving.
  • Mathematical Databases Building distinct datasets for training models on mathematical reasoning (e.g., AVID).
